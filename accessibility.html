Accessibility Statement / הצהרת נגישות

Clarifind is committed to providing an accessible, inclusive experience for all visitors, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet the requirements of Israeli Standard ת״י 5568 in alignment with WCAG 2.0 Level AA.

קלריפיינד מחויבת לספק חוויית שימוש נגישה ושוויונית לכל המבקרים, לרבות אנשים עם מוגבלויות. אנו פועלים לעמידה בדרישות התקן הישראלי ת״י 5568 ובהתאמה לרמת WCAG 2.0 AA.

What we did to improve accessibility

  • Added skip links, landmarks, and a consistent heading hierarchy.
  • Ensured full keyboard navigation with visible focus indicators.
  • Improved form labels, error messaging, and live status updates.
  • Checked color contrast and reduced motion for users who prefer it.

Supported environments

The site is designed to work on modern browsers (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari) on desktop and mobile devices, with responsive layouts supporting 200% zoom.

Known limitations

We are not aware of any remaining accessibility barriers. If you encounter an issue, please let us know and we will address it.

Third-party services

The contact form is powered by Formspree for secure submissions. Some project links open external sites that may have their own accessibility policies. If you encounter issues with a third-party service, contact us and we will provide an alternative.
