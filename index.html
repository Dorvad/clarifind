02 — Selected work

What I've built

A selection of projects across learning design, UX, and digital products.

01 Digital Platform · UX

Digital Learning Platform for Emissaries

Designed a mobile-friendly platform that helps emissaries quickly find what they need while working in the field. One clear place for content, tools, and updates.

The platform consolidates previously scattered resources into a single, structured experience. The design prioritizes fast navigation, clarity under pressure, and low cognitive load — because emissaries are often working in high-demand environments and don't have time to search.

→
Faster access to information and smoother day-to-day work for hundreds of users.
02 AI · Learning

AI Training Simulations

Designed AI-based training that lets participants practice real-life situations through guided conversations. From theory to practice — simply and interactively.

The simulation puts learners inside realistic scenarios where they have to respond, make decisions, and receive contextual feedback. Instead of passively reading about situations, participants actually practice them — in a safe, repeatable environment.

→
Planned for scale across large training groups.
03 VR · Experience Design

VR Learning Experience

Added clear guidance, storytelling, and structure to a VR experience — turning a confusing journey into a smooth, engaging one users can actually follow.

The original experience had compelling content but lacked the scaffolding to carry users through it effectively. I introduced narrative structure, orientation cues, and interaction design that made the VR environment feel intentional and learnable rather than overwhelming.

→
A confusing experience became a smooth, meaningful journey.
04 Systems · Operations

Escape Room Systems

Built internal tools for an escape room business — a staff knowledge portal and shift management system. Simplified operations and improved team coordination.

The tools were designed from the inside out — based on real operational needs, real friction points, and the way the team actually works. The result is a small but coherent system that reduced mistakes and made daily coordination less stressful.

→
Leaner operations, fewer errors, better team alignment.
05 Ongoing · Studio

Clarifind

An ongoing project where I build and test new ideas in learning, UX, and AI. Small apps, tools, and experiments — exploring better ways to design learning experiences.

Clarifind is both a brand and a practice — a place where ideas get tested before they become full products. Some experiments stay small, some grow into something real. The common thread is always the same: can we make this clearer? Can we make this easier to use?

Where curiosity meets craft.
