The process

From fuzzy to finished — in four moves.

I like simple, honest processes. No 12-phase methodology, no jargon-heavy decks. Just a clear path from your goal to a working solution.

🔍
1

Clarity call

15 minutes to hear what's going on, what you need, and whether I'm the right fit. No pitch, no pressure — just an honest look at the situation.

+10 XP · 15 min
🗺️
2

Diagnose & scope

I map out the learning challenge, identify what's missing, and lay out a realistic scope — so you know exactly what you're signing up for before anything starts.

+20 XP · 3–5 days
⚒️
3

Design & build

I design the structure, write the content, and build the learning experience — always checking in so nothing surprises you at the end.

+40 XP · 2–6 weeks
🏆
4

Launch & hand off

You get everything ready to use — tested, documented, and explained. I stick around to help you launch smoothly and answer anything that comes up.

+30 XP · Launch day 🚀
